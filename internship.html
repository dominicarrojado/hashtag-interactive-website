#HashtagInternProject

The Hashtag Interactive digital marketing internship program

The #HashtagInternProject gives interns the opportunity to plan and design an inbound marketing campaign for the agency and pitch their idea to management. If we think your Project is awesome enough, we may just execute your idea for real. Work with our Team to complete REAL client work including market research, copy writing, digital production and search engine optimisation efforts.

  • Develop inbound marketing content for Hashtag Interactive including social content, blog articles and press releases
  • One-on-one feedback and mentoring
  • Our internship programme is available during all semesters and has the potential to convert into a full-time opportunity

Being updated with current social trends. Coming up with cool campaigns. Analyzing data. Mastering digital marketing.

Job requirements

This is where most agencies say they're looking for marketing or communications students, but we're willing to take a chance on students pursuing areas of study other than those directly related to this position as long as they show an aptitude for digital marketing.

  • Proactive, energetic and creative with a love of learning - we'd pick an awesome attitude over straight A grades any day.
  • Strong creativity and MAD copywriting skills. And when we say MAD, we mean impeccable grammar and syntax with a flair for what's in the now.
  • Attention to detail and process driven to ensure completion of all tasks quickly and on time.
  • Must have great research skills and is able to analyse data.
  • We are looking for team members who embrace digital and love technology.
  • Graphic Design and Web Programming skills would be an extremely happy bonus.
